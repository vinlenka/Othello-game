Othello – rules

The Goal of the game:
Have more stones than the other player at the end of the game.

End of the game:
1.	When the board is full.
2.	When none of the players can make a valid move.

Move:
You can place your stone to any square in the grid as long as at least one of the opponent’s stones turns into your colour. 
The stone turns colour when it is surrounded from two sides with your colour in any direction.

Example: b w b will become b b b; or b w w w w b will turn to b b b b b b
